## Notes for System Implementation

### Key Points
1. **Second & Fourth Saturdays** are bank holidays (not working days)
2. **First, Third, Fifth Saturdays** are working days
3. **All Sundays** are holidays
4. **Religious holidays** may fall on any day of the week
5. **National holidays** are fixed dates

### Calculation Rules
- Business days = Working days only
- Skip: All Sundays + 2nd/4th Saturdays + National/Religious holidays
- Count: Monday-Friday (except holidays) + 1st/3rd/5th Saturdays
